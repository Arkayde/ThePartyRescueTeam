The Party Rescue Team are your personal chefs, bartenders, and service staff for all occasions that will execute a precise event of your dreams within a short notice. Every member of our staff is evaluated on the quality of their service, neatness, and presentation.

  • Personal Chef: A perfect help for your busy schedule and for people who are on the move and on a special diet or vegan. Chef to the rescue will come to your home and will prepare several dishes for a whole week or just for that special occasion.

  • Bartender: Our Bartender to the rescue will set up the bar, mix your drinks and pour drinks for you and your guests! A perfect helper for any special occasion at home party, an office party, etc.

  • Server: Our Server to the rescue is a multi tasker, and a team player. A perfect helper as your assistant in the kitchen or that special person that will display the food, replenish food, and even just for that friendly face that will pass your special hors d'oeuvres.
